Simpli Learn Info

Simplilearn was founded in 2009 by Krishna Kumar in Bangalore, India, with the aim of providing accessible and affordable online training programs in the rapidly growing digital economy. Kumar, who had worked in the IT industry for over a decade, realized that there was a significant gap between the skills required by the industry and the skills possessed by professionals.[2] The company started as a blog where Kumar shared his knowledge and expertise with a small community of learners.
